01

How you can use it

You can share it, adapt it, and make your own version.

You can copy, redistribute, remix, transform, and build on the Breathing Space system as long as you follow the license terms. You can make a version for another doorway, class, room, group, note, workshop, website, or personal practice.

If you fork the project, keep the breath structure visible: Take a breath before you ______ / You don't have to ______.

02

License terms

Credit, noncommercial use, same license.

  1. Attribution. Credit Breathing Space, link back to the project when possible, and say if you changed it.
  2. NonCommercial. Do not use the work for commercial purposes.
  3. ShareAlike. If you remix or adapt the work, share your version under the same license.
  4. No additional restrictions. Do not add legal or technical restrictions that prevent other people from using the work in ways the license allows.

03

Care around stories

Fork the system without extracting the person.

The structure of Breathing Space is open. The care around a person's experience still matters. If someone shares a story, keep their chosen credit, privacy, and context intact.

A fork should make more room for people to breathe, not turn vulnerability into material.

Creative Commons lets the object travel while keeping credit and care attached.
