What stays open

The system is open: the structure, the prompt form, and the idea of taking a breath before entering. You can translate it, fork it, rewrite it, or use it in another room, website, workshop, or note.

What stays careful

If you share or adapt it, credit Breathing Space, link the license, note if you changed anything, and keep the same license attached. If someone contributes a story, keep their privacy, context, and chosen credit intact.
