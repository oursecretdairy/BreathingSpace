Breathing Space Door Manual

Leave this on a door and invite one small pause.

Breathing Space starts right before entering. This sheet can be taped to a door, hallway, classroom, studio, or shared threshold so people can meet the breath in the place where it happens.

How to use it

Place it where someone is about to cross.

  1. Choose a real entryway: a door, room, studio, hallway, or meeting space.
  2. Tape or pin this sheet where it can be read before entering.
  3. Let someone fill in the two-line breath in their own words.
  4. Keep it to one moment, not a whole story.
  5. Leave it there long enough for someone else to use it too.
